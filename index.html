It brings the spotlight back on your business.

The stages involve a carefully crafted combination of Online and Onsite Engagements with the enterprise. The modules are elaborately designed to sequentially address the potential achievement of your enterprise. A participant has to undertake the program in the sequence it is prescribed and cannot be subscribed in any other order.









PROGRAM DELIVERY

The modules are delivered through a combination of online and offline engagements. It has a logical sequence and hence adopts a stage-wise framework. It focuses on the four independent and cyclical parameters of every business to assess the potential. It takes into consideration Leadership, Capacity, Customer and Opportunity as the four main ingredients. Leadership and Capacity are internal to an enterprise whereas Customers and Opportunity are external. It further breaks the cycle into Competence and Performance related parameters.

The evaluation process deploys deep study of these four main pillars of your business and evaluates them individually at first and then holistically to arrive at the final score. A lot of time is spent on discussions, interviews and studying these so as to unearth all the aspects which are hindering growth. The collected data is also run through a very intelligent CRM system for errors. The final output is a comprehensive cause-effect analysis with a potential scorecard.









Xcelerate is the product of years of experience, hands-on working with clients and deep divining into their issues & suggesting successful remedial actions. It’s the breaking down of challenges enterprises face into recognizable factors, and suggesting feasible solutions to remove them. The program is interactive and involves various levels of engagements to bring out the real bottlenecks, backed by data, feedback and trends.

The Enterprise Potential Scorecard

Every participating enterprise receives a potential score card at the completion of the program. Inputs collected from the exercise is used to derive the participants “Enterprise Potential Scorecard”. This score is generated using the data and running them through a variety of parameters to arrive at the final score. This compares your Potential rating visa vis the industry standards and also plots it amongst the industry peers. A high or low potential score indicates the future health of the enterprise, and strategic conclusions can be made based on these.

It offers a scientific and measurable approach to quantify the quality of your business. It works on four major principles- Leadership, Capacity, Customer, and Opportunity to discover your unrealized enterprise potential.
